ABOUT ME

PROFILE

I am a strongly motivated artist who believes that music brings joy, passion and solidarity to people worldwide. I have studied music internationally, in programs in Spain, Morocco, Britain, Italy and Jerusalem. Through hard work and dedication, I have been chosen to perform in concerts in UK, around Italy, Israel/Palestine, Spain, Sahara Dessert and Bucharest among other countries. I am an enthusiastic and energetic person who can't wait to collaborate and work in the music industry delivering the true values of music to people all around the world!
